Dr. Muhammad Jamil

Post Doc

Dr. Muhammad Jamil
Dr. Muhammad Jamil received his Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University in 2014. He worked as postdoctoral research fellow in NUS till Dec 2016. Was actively involved in development of a novel method to quantify mitral regurgitation in the human heart. Worked collaboratively with clinicians, surgeons and peer researchers on acquisition, development and analysis of the novel algorithm. Was actively involved in simulation and experimental validation of the cardiovascular flows relating to brain, embryonic flows and human heart. He joined Koc University Biofluids Lab in Mechanical Engineering Department in Jan 2017. The scope of research involves simulation and experimental validation of cardiovascular flows, surgical planning, analysis and development of robust cardiovascular medical devices tailored for optimal performance in clinical settings.
