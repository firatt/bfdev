Welcome


Welcome to the Prof. Kerem Pekkan's Biofluids and Cardiovascular Mechanics Laboratory in the Departments of Mechanical Engineering at the Koç University.

Through the research we have been conducting in our laboratory for 6 years at Koç University, we are working on devices for cardiovascular biomechanics and child heart diseases. What we try to do in the framework of the European Research Concuil project, one of the most prestigious scientific programs in Europe, has a lot of clues about the innovations in this area.

Principally, we develop computer programs designed to predict the changes that veins have during the disease or due to normal growth, both at the macro and micro level. We compare the predictive capacity of these programs with our observations in the temporal congenital heart disease models that we temporarily created in the chicken embryo. We try to understand the genetic and structural changes that occur when we eliminate the mechanical effects that create the disease. We also develop artificial heart pumps that do not require additional energy for child heart patients, and life support unit cannulas for the newborn.
